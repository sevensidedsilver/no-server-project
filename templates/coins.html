There's More To The Market Than Just Bitcoin

Litecoin (LTC)

Introduced to the market in 2011, Litecoin was inspired by, and is nearly technically identical to, Bitcoin. It's actually a fork of Bitcoin - differening only by having a decreased block generation time, increased maximum number of coins, and a new hashing algorithm.

Ethereum (ETH)

Ethereum does more than it's crytocurrency token - the ether. It's a blockchain based development platform which allows for decentralized hosting of applications. Originally, Ether was only used to compensate node admins for their computational contributions.

Bitcoin (BTC)

The giant that started it all - Bitcoin has maintained it's status as the most influencial and popular CryptoCurrency with ease. No one knows for sure who's responsible for the software - which was released open source on Github in 2009.
